Dua When Waking From Sleep

When the Noble Prophet (S.M) would awaken from His rest (sleep), He would read:

اَلْحَمْدُ للهِ الَّذِىْ اَحْىَ نَفْسِىْ بَعْدَ مَااَمَاتَهَا وَ اِلَيْهِ النُّشُوْرُ

English: Alhumdu Lil Laahil Lazee Ahyaa Nafsee Ba’da Maa Amaataha Wa ilaihin Nushoor

Meaning: "All praise is due to Al’laah, Who has granted us life after taking it away from us and towards Him is our resurrection"

Dua Before Eating

Read this Dua before eating:

بِسْمِ اللهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيْمِ

English: Bismil laahir Rahmaanir Raheem

Meaning: Al’laah’s Name we begin with, The Compassionate, Most Merciful.

If you Forgotten to read it and remember after completing your half meal then say,

بِسْمِ اللهِ اَوَّلَهُ وَاَخِرَهُ

English: Bismil laahi Aw’walahu Wa Aakhirahu

Meaning: "Al’laah’s Name we begin and end with"

Dua after Eating

Hazrat Abu Sa’eed Khudri(R.A) reported that the Noble Prophet(S.M) would read the following dua after he had completed his meal:

اَ لْحَمْدُ للهِ الَّذِىْ اَطْعَمَنَا وَسَقَاناَ وَجَعَلَناَ مِنَ الْمُسْلِمِيْنَ

English: Alhumdu lil laahil Lazee At’amana Wa Saqaana Wa Ja’alana Minal Muslimeen

Meaning: "All Praise is due to Al’laah, who has blessed us with food and drink and made us from amongst the Believers"

What To Do When You Sneeze

Hazrat Abu Hurairah(R.A) reports that the Noble Prophet (S.M) said, “When anyone from amongst you sneezes, then he should say,

اَ لْحَمْدُ للهِ

English: Alhumdu lil laah

Meaning: "All Praise is due to Al’laah"

And his friend or brother who hears him should say,

يْرحَمُكَ اللهُ

English: Yarhamukal’laah

Meaning: "May Almighty Al’laah have mercy upon you"

Then in response to this, the one who sneezed should say,

يَهْدِيْكُمُ اللهُ وَ يُصْلِحُ بَالَكُمْ

English: Yahdee kumul’laahu wa Yuslihoo Baalakum

Meaning: "Al’laah grant you guidance and remedy your condition."

Dua in Distress or Discomfort

لآَ اِلَهَ اِلاَّ اللهُ الْحَلِيْمُ الْحَكِيْمُ لآَ اِلَهَ اِلاَّ اللهُ رَبُّ الْعَرْشِ الْعَظِيْمِ لآَ اِلَهَ اِلاَّ اللهُ رَبُّ السَّمَاوَاتَ وَاْلاَرْضِ وَرَبُّ الْعَرْشِ الْكَرِيْمِ ط

English: Laa ilaaha il’lal laahul Haleemul Hakeemu – Laa ilaaha il’lal laahu Rab’bul Arshil Azeem – Laa ilaaha il’lal laahu Rab’bus Samawaati wal Ardi wa Rab’bul Arshil Kareem

Meaning: "There is none worthy of worship except Al’laah, The Fore-bearing, The All Wise. There is none worthy of worship except Al’laah, The Lord of the Exalted Throne. There is none worthy of worship except Al’laah, The Lord of the Skies and The Lord of the Earth and the Lord of the distinguished Throne."

Ayatul Kursi

English: Allahu la ilaha illa huwa Al -Haiyul-Qaiyum La ta'khudhuhu sinatun wa la nawm lahu ma fi as-samawati wa ma fil-'ard Man dhal-ladhi yashfa'u 'indahu illa bi-idhnihi Ya'lamu ma bayna aydihim wa ma khalfahum wa la yuhituna bi shai'in min 'ilmihi illa bima sha'a Wasi'a kursiyuhus-samawati wal ard wa la ya'uduhu hifdhuhuma wa Hu wal 'Aliyul-Adheem

Meaning: Allah! There is no god but He - the Living, The Self-subsisting, Eternal. No slumber can seize Him Nor Sleep. His are all things In the heavens and on earth. Who is there can intercede In His presence except As he permitteth? He knoweth what (appeareth to His creatures As) Before or After or Behind them. Nor shall they compass Aught of his knowledge Except as He willeth. His throne doth extend Over the heavens And on earth, and He feeleth No fatigue in guarding And preserving them, For He is the Most High. The Supreme (in glory)

Dua For Iftar

اللَّهُمَّ اِنِّى لَكَ صُمْتُ وَبِكَ امنْتُ [وَعَلَيْكَ تَوَكَّلْتُ] وَعَلَى رِزْقِكَ اَفْطَرْتُ

English: Allahumma Inni laka sumtu wa bika amantu [wa alayka tawakkalto] wa ‘ala rizqika aftartu

Meaning: "O Allah! I fasted for You and I believe in You [and I put my trust in You] and I break my fast with Your sustenance."

note:

["wa ‘alayka tawakkaltu" is quoted in some books of knowledge - but not all, hence it is in brackets]

Surah Fatiha

1. Bismillāhi r-raḥmāni r-raḥīm

2. Al ḥamdu lillāhi rabbi l-’ālamīn

3. Ar raḥmāni r-raḥīm

4. Māliki yawmi d-dīn

5. Iyyāka na’budu wa iyyāka nasta’īn

6. Ihdinā ṣ-ṣirāṭ al-mustaqīm

7. Ṣirāṭ al-laḏīna an’amta ‘alayhim ġayril maġḍūbi ‘alayhim walāḍ ḍāllīn

Translation

1. In the name of God, the Lord of Mercy, the Giver of Mercy!

2. Praise belongs to God, Lord of the Worlds,

3. the Lord of Mercy, the Giver of Mercy,

4. Master of the Day of Judgement.

5. It is You we worship; it is You we ask for help

6. Guide us to the straight path

7. the path of those You have blessed, those who incur no anger and who have not gone astray.
